STORY OF KEROVERSE

After eons of protecting the SOL Shards from foreign invaders, the ancient KEROS finally rest, and replace themselves by building the artificially intelligent KEROBOTS. Efficient, ruthless and completely invulnerable, the KEROBOTS became an unstoppable force of nature.

However, just when peace began to feel like the new normal to the KEROS, a human time capsule found its way into the Keroverse, wreaking havoc into this pocket dimension.
